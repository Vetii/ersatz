The boolean xor operation

Derivation of the Tseitin transformation:

O ≡ A ⊕ B
O ≡ ((¬A & B) | (A & ¬B))
(O → ((¬A & B) | (A & ¬B))) & (¬O → ¬((¬A & B) | (A & ¬B)))

Left hand side:

O → ((¬A & B) | (A & ¬B))
¬O | ((¬A & B) | (A & ¬B))
¬O | ((¬A | A) & (¬A | ¬B) & (A | B) & (¬B | B))
¬O | ((¬A | ¬B) & (A | B))
(¬O | ¬A | ¬B) & (¬O | A | B)

Right hand side:

¬O → ¬((¬A & B) | (A & ¬B))
O | ¬((¬A & B) | (A & ¬B))
O | (¬(¬A & B) & ¬(A & ¬B))
O | ((A | ¬B) & (¬A | B))
(O | ¬A | B) & (O | A | ¬B)

Result:

(¬O | ¬A | ¬B) & (¬O | A | B) & (O | ¬A | B) & (O | A | ¬B)

The boolean else-then-if or mux operation

Derivation of the Tseitin transformation:

O ≡ (F & ¬P) | (T & P)
(O → ((F & ¬P) | (T & P))) & (¬O → ¬((F & ¬P) | (T & P)))

Left hand side:

O → ((F & ¬P) | (T & P))
¬O | ((F & ¬P) | (T & P))
¬O | ((F | T) & (F | P) & (T | ¬P) & (¬P | P))
¬O | ((F | T) & (F | P) & (T | ¬P))
(¬O | F | T) & (¬O | F | P) & (¬O | T | ¬P)

Right hand side:

¬O → ¬((F & ¬P) | (T & P))
O | ¬((F & ¬P) | (T & P))
O | (¬(F & ¬P) & ¬(T & P))
O | ((¬F | P) & (¬T | ¬P))
(O | ¬F | P) & (O | ¬T | ¬P)

Result:

(¬O | F | T) & (¬O | F | P) & (¬O | T | ¬P) & (O | ¬F | P) & (O | ¬T | ¬P)
